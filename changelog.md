Large update to coincide with a new pvp focused update on Dystoria.

## Additions
- Battle HUD
  - The existing pokeball displays have been upgraded into an entire battle HUD.
  - The HUD works entirely clientside, making Dystoria Tweaks a clean alternative to other battle HUD mods.
  - Using the HUD on Dystoria itself will let it connect to other items such as the Team Sheets and provide additional information.
- Config menu
  - All features of Tweaks are now togglable in a Mod Menu config menu.
